Planetary Facts

Mars is known as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance, caused by iron oxide (rust) on its surface.

Atmospheric Composition

Mars' atmosphere is mostly carbon dioxide (95.3%), with nitrogen and argon as the next most common gases.

Moons of Mars

Mars has two small moons, Phobos and Deimos, which are likely captured asteroids from the asteroid belt.

Geological Features

Mars has diverse geological features, including the largest volcano in the Solar System, Olympus Mons, and a massive canyon system, Valles Marineris.

Exploration Missions

Mars has been extensively explored by missions like Viking, Mars rovers (Spirit, Opportunity, Curiosity, Perseverance), and orbiters such as Mars Odyssey and Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter.
