Planetary Facts

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life.

Atmospheric Composition

The Earth's atmosphere is composed of 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and trace amounts of other gases.

The Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite and is the fifth largest moon in the Solar System.

Geological Features

Earth has a diverse geology, including mountains, valleys, and plains, shaped by geological processes.

Exploration Missions

Many missions have explored Earth, including satellites like Landsat and missions from various space agencies.
