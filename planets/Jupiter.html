Planetary Facts

Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System and is known for its Great Red Spot, a giant storm.

Atmospheric Composition

Jupiter's atmosphere is composed mostly of hydrogen and helium, with trace amounts of other gases.

Moons of Jupiter

Jupiter has 79 known moons, including the four largest: Ganymede, Io, Europa, and Callisto, known as the Galilean moons.

Geological Features

Jupiter has no solid surface. It is a gas giant, with bands of clouds and a dynamic atmosphere.

Exploration Missions

Jupiter has been explored by missions such as Pioneer, Voyager, Galileo, and Juno, revealing much about its atmosphere and moons.
