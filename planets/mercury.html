Planetary Facts

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and has the shortest orbit around it.

Atmospheric Composition

Mercury's atmosphere is very thin and consists mostly of oxygen, sodium, and hydrogen.

No Moons

Mercury has no natural satellites or moons.

Geological Features

Mercury's surface is heavily cratered, similar to our Moon, and has massive cliffs formed by planetary contraction.

Exploration Missions

Mercury has been explored by Mariner 10 and the MESSENGER mission, revealing much about its surface and composition.
